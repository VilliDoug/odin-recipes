Pasta Cacio e Pepe

A simple yet tasteful choice when making pasta at home! Pecorino cheese adds an exquisite flavour to the dish.

One can find Pecorino cheese at select import supermarkets, or online if needed.

For our Cacio e Pepe, we will need the following

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti of choice (other choices like Penne regate or whatever else you like are also fine)
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Pecorino cheese (or parmesan/grana padano if you need an alternative)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Pepare the water for boiling your pasta
  2. Grate a good amount of Pecorino cheese, between 50-100 grams, and set aside
  3. Grind the pepper and add into a mildy hot pan, without oil, and lighty roast it
  4. Add water onto the pepper and cook it for a few minutes - this helps infuse the water with flavour from the peppers
  5. Add "cooking water" to your pecorino cheese and mix with a fork or whisker if you have one (slowly!) This will make your cheese melt and turn into a sort of cream. Keep the temperature below 70-75C otherwise the protein content of the cheese will clot and your mix won't be smooth.
  6. 2 minutes or so before the pasta reaches it's "cooking time", remove it and add the same into the pan which contains the pepper
  7. Mix well and slowly add cooking water to avoid it frying instead of cooking
  8. Add the pecorino crema onto your pasta, and keep mixing to achieve a creamy texture. Remember to add cooking water as needed
  9. Voila!
