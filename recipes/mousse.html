Coffee Mousse

Caffeine is one hell of a drug

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Double shot of espresso
  • 1/4 cup of confectioners sugar
  • 120g of melted and cooled dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form
  2. In a separate bowl, combine milk, cooled coffee, sugar and melted chocolate
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the coffee-chocolate mixture
  4. Divide the mousse into serving glasses or bowls and refrigerate for at least two hours, or until set
