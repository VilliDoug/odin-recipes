Anchovy and Caper Pasta

Mmm...lovely plain spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Can of Anchovies
  • 1 Small Can of Capers
  • 1 Packet of Spaghetti
  • Salt - for boiling the pasta
  • Italian Parsley - to garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the butter, anchovies and capers until mostly homogenous, then cover and set aside;
  2. Prepare the water for boiling your spaghetti, and add a good amount of salt to it when it boils;
  3. A minute or so before your pasta's cooking time is reached, remove pasta and add into a separate pan in order to mix
  4. Add the butter mix into the pasta, as well as "cooking water" when necessary, and mix well until evenly distributed and with a creamy texture;
  5. Serve and add the parsley as garnish if desired.
