Affogato

Affogato is a rather simple dessert, but the blend of the sweetness of the gelato and the aroma of the espresso make it a great choice after meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of gelato, typically vanilla or fior-di-latte
  • Single or Double shot of espresso

Steps

  1. After adding the scoop of gelato into a cup or bowl, simply pour the espresso shots over it.
  2. Optionally, it could served separate at the table and have them poured right before eating.
